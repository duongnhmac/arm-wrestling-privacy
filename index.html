1. Introduction

Welcome to Arm Wrestling Pro. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. This document outlines how we handle your data and the rules for using our training application.

2. Privacy Policy (Data Collection)

Local Storage: Arm Wrestling Pro is designed to be a privacy-first application. All your training history, progress statistics, and app settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We do not transmit your workout data to any external servers.

Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers. The app does not require a user account to function.

Third-Party Services: Our app may use standard platform services (like Apple App Store or Google Play Store services) which may collect anonymous usage data for stability and performance monitoring.

4. Safety Guidelines

For your safety, we recommend:

  • Never skip the warm-up session.
  • Stop immediately if you feel "sharp" or "stinging" pain in your elbow, wrist, or shoulder.
  • Keep your shoulder in line with your arm to avoid "the break arm" position.
